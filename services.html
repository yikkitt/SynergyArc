The Real Cost Comparison

It's not just the material cost. It's the total job.

When clients compare Enboard to plywood, they compare material prices. But material is only part of what you pay for. Here's what a full cost comparison actually looks like.

Plywood Build Enboard Build
Material costLowerSimilar or slightly higher
Labour (setup)Higher: screws, drilling, carpentryLower: slot-fit, smaller crew
Setup timeFull dayHalf day
Teardown & disposalHigher: skip hire, disposal feesLow: cardboard is recycled
ESG data reportNoYes, included
Reusable componentsRarelyPartially — modular panels
Total project costHigher than it looksCloser than you think

On a typical 60m² activation, the labour and teardown savings from Enboard can offset 60–80% of the material price difference. Message us with your brief and we'll run the exact numbers for your project.
