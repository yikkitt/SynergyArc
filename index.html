Your Path, Your Pace

Start with one piece. Grow from there.

You don't have to overhaul your entire event to make an impact. Most clients start with one sustainable swap — and build from there. Every stage moves your brand forward.

01
Baby Step
Replace one piece

Swap just your backdrop or entrance arch with Enboard. Keep everything else conventional. Zero risk — you'll see the quality firsthand before going further. Your stakeholders see the story. Your event looks identical.

Real example Cardboard backdrop + conventional PVC banners + standard foamboard signs. One event. One switch. A sustainability data report included.

02
Hybrid
Mix & match materials

High-visibility pieces go Enboard — backdrops, arches, furniture. High-volume items like stickers and small banners stay conventional. Best of both worlds, full visual impact, and a stronger sustainability story.

Real example Enboard entrance arch + Enboard backdrop + RPET fabric banner + conventional foamboard signs. 50/50 split. Identical visual impact.
